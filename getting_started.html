Basic Implementation

Single handed indication of up, down, left, right.. zoom in and out.

Starting in neutral
A neutral position with Jazz hands is the position where no navigation event is triggered. Keeping your hand in one of the six "zones" triggers a timer-based event to fire.
To continue, hold a single hand to the behind the controller, towards the screen.
Navigation zones
Moving along the X, Y and Z axis allows for 6-degrees of movement.
Implementation
A basic implementation just an init and a binding to navigation. The source is available on github.
